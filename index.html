Welcome!

This is the official website of the Marek Grzybowski Research Group (Group 10A) at the Institute of Organic Chemistry, Polish Academy of Sciences.
We design and investigate advanced π-conjugated systems, where aromatic and antiaromatic character plays a central role in governing their photophysical properties. Our group combines synthetic organic chemistry with spectroscopy and theory to explore new classes of functional fluorophores.

  • Analogues of polycyclic arenes containing main-group elements
  • Low-molecular-weight near-infrared (NIR) fluorophores
  • Fluorescent macrocycles
